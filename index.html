Why Come to Chengdu for Medical Care?

Chengdu is home to Sichuan University's West China Hospital — one of the top hospitals in China, ranked among the best in the world for several specialties including respiratory medicine, neurology, and digestive surgery.

The West China School of Stomatology is the #1 dental hospital in China, offering implants, orthodontics, and oral surgery at a fraction of US dental prices.

Add in the Chengdu University of TCM for acupuncture and traditional medicine, and you have a complete medical destination — all in a safe, modern, and friendly city famous for its pandas and cuisine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about medical travel to Chengdu.

Is it safe to have medical treatment in China? ▼

Yes. China's top-tier hospitals (like West China Hospital) meet international standards. Many have JCI accreditation. Doctors at major hospitals are highly trained, often with international experience. Chengdu is a safe, modern city.

Do the doctors speak English? ▼

Some doctors at international departments speak English. But for complex medical discussions, having a dedicated translator (that's me) ensures nothing gets lost in translation. I'll be with you in every appointment.

How do I get a visa for medical travel? ▼

US citizens can apply for a medical visa (S签证) or a tourist visa (L签证) depending on your treatment length. I will help you obtain the necessary hospital invitation letter and guide you through the application. China has been making visa processes easier for US travelers.

How do I pay? Do you accept insurance? ▼

I accept payment via PayPal, bank transfer, or wire transfer. Some international departments at Chengdu's top hospitals may accept international insurance with direct billing — I can help verify your coverage and confirm with the hospital before you arrive. For self-pay patients, treatment costs are low enough that many choose to pay out of pocket.

What if something goes wrong during treatment? ▼

I'm your local advocate. I'll help communicate with doctors, resolve any issues, and ensure you're cared for. China's top hospitals have patient complaint systems and medical dispute resolution processes. I'll be by your side through the entire process.

Can I combine medical treatment with tourism? ▼

Absolutely! Chengdu is home to the famous Giant Panda Base, incredible Sichuan cuisine, historic sites like Jinli Ancient Street, and stunning natural scenery. Many patients spend a few extra days exploring. I can help arrange tours too.

How long do I need to stay in Chengdu? ▼

It depends on your treatment. A checkup or dental work: 3-5 days. Surgery: 1-3 weeks including recovery. IVF: 2-3 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline before you come.
