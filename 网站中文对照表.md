Frequently Asked Questions → 常见问题
Everything you need to know about medical travel to Chengdu.
→ 关于来成都看病你需要知道的一切。

Q: Is it safe to have medical treatment in China?
→ 问：在中国看病安全吗？
A: Yes. China's top-tier hospitals meet international standards. Many have JCI accreditation. Chengdu is a safe, modern city.
→ 答：安全。中国顶级医院达到国际标准，很多有JCI认证。成都是安全现代化的城市。

Q: Do the doctors speak English?
→ 问：医生会说英语吗？
A: Some doctors at international departments speak English. I'll be with you in every appointment.
→ 答：国际部的部分医生会说英语。每次就诊我都会在你身边翻译。

Q: How do I get a visa for medical travel?
→ 问：怎么申请医疗签证？
A: US citizens can apply for a medical visa or tourist visa. I'll provide the hospital invitation letters and guide you.
→ 答：美国公民可申请S签证（医疗签证）或L签证（旅游签证）。我会提供医院邀请函并指导你。

Q: How do I pay? Do you accept insurance?
→ 问：怎么付款？接受保险吗？
A: PayPal, bank transfer, or wire transfer. Major hospitals accept international insurance with direct billing.
→ 答：PayPal、银行转账。大医院接受国际保险直付。

Q: What if something goes wrong during treatment?
→ 问：治疗出问题怎么办？
A: I'm your local advocate. I'll help communicate with doctors and resolve issues.
→ 答：我是你的本地代理人。我帮你和医生沟通、解决问题。

Q: Can I combine medical treatment with tourism?
→ 问：能不能顺便旅游？
A: Absolutely! Chengdu is home to the Giant Panda Base, incredible Sichuan cuisine, and historic sites.
→ 答：当然可以！成都有大熊猫基地、绝美的川菜和古迹。

Q: How long do I need to stay in Chengdu?
→ 问：需要待多久？
A: Checkup/dental: 3-5 days. Surgery: 1-3 weeks. IVF: 2-3 weeks.
→ 答：体检/牙科：3-5天。手术：1-3周。试管婴儿：2-3周。
