1. Definitions

Pandacare Medical (“we”, “us”) is an independent concierge service operating in Chengdu, Sichuan, People’s Republic of China.

Client (“you”) is the individual receiving services.

Services means the non-medical concierge services described in Section 2.

Medical Provider means any hospital, clinic, or healthcare professional providing diagnosis or treatment to the Client.

2. Scope of Services

Pandacare Medical provides NON-MEDICAL concierge services only:

  • Hospital / doctor recommendation based on described needs
  • Appointment scheduling and registration assistance
  • Airport pickup and drop-off
  • Hotel booking assistance
  • In-person interpretation and translation during medical visits
  • Private vehicle and driver arrangement
  • Medical records translation
  • Local guidance and logistical support
We do NOT provide: medical diagnosis, treatment, advice, prescriptions, or procedures.
We are a logistical facilitator and translator — not a healthcare provider.

  • After services start: no refund for unused days

Pandacare Medical is NOT a medical provider. We make no warranties regarding medical quality, treatment outcomes, or Provider competence. Our total liability is limited to the total fees you paid.

We are not liable for: medical complications, adverse outcomes, misdiagnosis, personal property loss, or indirect damages. You travel to China voluntarily and assume all associated risks. We strongly recommend you obtain travel insurance and medical insurance.

6. Client Responsibilities

Provide accurate medical history. Follow hospital rules. Pay medical fees directly to the Provider. Obtain appropriate visa and travel documents. Treat our staff with respect.

7. Privacy

Your health information is kept confidential and shared only as necessary to provide Services. We do not sell your data.

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the People’s Republic of China.

Any dispute shall be resolved through good-faith negotiation first (30 days). If unresolved, the dispute shall be submitted to the competent court in Chengdu, Sichuan Province, PRC.

We are not liable for failure due to events beyond our control: natural disasters, epidemics, government actions, war, visa denial, airline cancellations, etc.

If any term is unenforceable, the remainder stays in effect. This Agreement is the complete understanding between the parties.
